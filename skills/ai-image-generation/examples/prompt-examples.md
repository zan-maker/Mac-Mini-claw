## 1. Product Visualization

### Electronics & Tech
```
[Product name] on a [surface/material] background, [lighting conditions], 
professional product photography, studio lighting, clean composition, 
highly detailed, 8K resolution, commercial use
```

**Examples:**
- "iPhone 15 Pro on a marble surface, soft studio lighting, professional product photography, clean composition, highly detailed, 8K resolution"
- "MacBook Pro open on a wooden desk with coffee cup, morning light through window, lifestyle product shot, detailed, professional photography"

### Fashion & Apparel
```
[Clothing item] on a model, [setting/background], [lighting], 
fashion photography, editorial style, detailed textures, 
vibrant colors, professional shoot, full body
```

**Examples:**
- "Leather jacket on a model in an urban setting, golden hour lighting, fashion photography, editorial style, detailed textures, professional shoot"
- "Running shoes on feet during a trail run, action shot, dynamic lighting, sports photography, detailed, vibrant colors"

## Prompt Engineering Tips

### 1. **Be Specific**
- Bad: "a dog"
- Good: "a golden retriever puppy playing in a sunlit garden, detailed fur, happy expression"

### 2. **Include Style References**
- "in the style of Studio Ghibli"
- "photorealistic, 8K resolution"
- "minimalist vector illustration"

### 3. **Specify Composition**
- "close-up shot"
- "wide angle landscape"
- "top-down view"
- "dynamic angle"

### 4. **Add Lighting & Atmosphere**
- "golden hour lighting"
- "moody atmospheric"
- "studio lighting"
- "natural daylight"

### 5. **Include Technical Details**
- "1024x1024 pixels"
- "square aspect ratio"
- "high detail"
- "professional photography"

### 6. **Consider Use Case**
- "works as social media post"
- "usable in UI design"
- "suitable for print"
- "optimized for web"
