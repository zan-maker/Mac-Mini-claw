## 🤔 Possible Reasons for Failure

### 1. **Incorrect Company Names/Tickers**
- **USA.AX** might not be a valid ASX ticker for a gold company
- **LAT.AX** might not be a valid ASX ticker for a copper company
- ASX tickers are typically 3 letters, not 3 letters + ".AX"

### 2. **Hypothetical Examples in Report**
- The mining report might contain AI-generated hypothetical companies
- Other companies in the report (AUG.V, CBM.V, SVR.V, CAN.AX) were found successfully
- These two might be placeholders or examples

### 3. **Name Variations**
- Companies might be listed under different names
- "US Gold Limited" could be "U.S. Gold Corp" (ticker: USAU)
- "Latin Copper Co" could be a subsidiary or differently named entity

### 4. **Data Source Issues**
- The mining report source data might have errors
- Tickers might be from different exchanges (TSX, OTC) not ASX
