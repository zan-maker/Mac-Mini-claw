**Research Date:** 2026-02-13
**Focus:** Companies selling AI agents/employees with per-agent monthly pricing OR outcome-based revenue share

## Executive Summary

The AI agent market has evolved into two primary pricing models:

1. **Per-Agent/Per-Month Pricing** - Subscription-based, predictable costs
2. **Outcome-Based Pricing** - Pay-per-result, revenue share models

## Detailed Pricing Analysis

### Usage-Based Pricing Factors

**Token/API-Based:**
- GPT-4: ~$0.03/1K input tokens, $0.06/1K output tokens
- Typical conversation: 500-2,000 tokens ($0.015-$0.12)

**Message/Conversation-Based:**
- Range: $0.05-$0.50 per conversation
- 10K monthly conversations = $500-$5,000/month

**Action-Based:**
- Simple: $0.01-$0.02/action
- Complex API: $0.10+/action
- 50K monthly actions = $500-$5,000/month

### Subscription Tier Ranges

- **Starter:** $0-$50/month (1K-5K interactions)
- **Professional:** $50-$500/month (10K-50K interactions)
- **Enterprise:** $500+/month (100K+ interactions)

### Development Costs

- **No-Code Setup:** $0-$200
- **Low-Code:** $1,000-$10,000
- **Custom Development:** $10,000-$100,000+
- **Maintenance:** $500-$5,000/month

## Key Insights

### Why AI Agent Pricing is Difficult

1. **Workload Scope Shifts** - Each agent task varies based on context
2. **Usage Scales Asymmetrically** - Same task, different resource usage per user
3. **Value â‰  Willingness-to-Pay** - Buyers may not value what agents deliver

### Three Value Axes for Pricing

1. **Value Attribution** - Can customers tie outputs to outcomes?
2. **Execution Autonomy** - How independent is the agent?
3. **Workload Predictability** - How spiky is the usage?

## Notable Companies Not Using Traditional Models

### Outcome-Based Pioneers

1. **Intercom** - $0.99 per resolved ticket (pioneering outcome pricing)
2. **Custom GPT implementations** - Usage-based, often 60-80% cheaper at scale

### Emerging Trends

- Moving away from "per seat" (agents replace seats)
- Hybrid models becoming standard
- Outcome-based gaining traction for quantifiable results
- Usage-based for variable workload scenarios
