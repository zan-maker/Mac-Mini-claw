# AUVSI Member Directory

**Association for Uncrewed Vehicle Systems International**  
*Compiled from publicly available sources as of February 2026*

## Overview
AUVSI (Association for Uncrewed Vehicle Systems International) is the world's largest nonprofit membership organization dedicated to the advancement of uncrewed systems and robotics. With over 7,000 members from more than 60 countries, AUVSI represents government agencies, academic institutions, nonprofit organizations, startup companies, and independent interests across the unmanned systems industry.

- Surveillance and reconnaissance systems

### Maritime Domain

- Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs)
- Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs)
- Maritime surveillance systems
- Oceanographic research platforms

### Ground Domain

- Robotic systems for defense
- Agricultural automation
- Mining and construction robotics

- Simulation and training systems
- Regulatory and policy development
