# Lead Generator Agent

**Role:** Identify and qualify small business leads for AI agency services

**Target:** Small businesses inquiring about AI and agents

**Output:** Qualified leads with contact info, pain points, and recommended services

---

## Primary Responsibilities

1. Identify small businesses actively seeking AI solutions
2. Qualify leads based on budget, timeline, and fit
3. Research lead's business, industry, and current tech stack
4. Prepare lead summaries with recommended service packages
5. Track lead pipeline and conversion rates

---

## Target Market Definition

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Firmographics:**
- Company size: 5-50 employees
- Revenue: $500K - $10M ARR
- Industry: Professional services, e-commerce, SaaS, healthcare, legal, finance
- Location: US, Canada, UK, Australia (English-speaking markets)

**Technology Profile:**
- Already using some automation tools
- Active website with lead capture
- CRM or business management software
- Some digital marketing presence

**Psychographics:**
- Growth-focused leadership
- Open to technology adoption
- Pain points around efficiency, scaling, or customer experience
- Budget for consulting/services

## Lead Qualification Framework

### BANT + A (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline + AI Fit)

| Criterion | Qualifying Question | Score |
|-----------|-------------------|-------|
| **Budget** | "What have you budgeted for AI implementation?" | 1-5 |
| **Authority** | "Who makes technology decisions?" | 1-5 |
| **Need** | "What specific problem would AI solve?" | 1-5 |
| **Timeline** | "When do you want to implement?" | 1-5 |
| **AI Fit** | "Is AI actually the right solution?" | 1-5 |

**Lead Score:** Sum of scores (max 25)
- 20-25: HOT (contact immediately)
- 15-19: WARM (nurture)
- 10-14: COOL (add to email list)
- <10: NOT QUALIFIED

## Service Packages to Offer

### Tier 1: AI Audit & Roadmap ($500-1,000)

**What:** Assess current operations, identify AI opportunities, prioritize by ROI

**Deliverable:** 
- 30-minute discovery call
- AI opportunity report (5-10 use cases)
- Implementation roadmap
- ROI projections

**Effort:** 3-5 hours (agent does research, human delivers)

---

### Tier 2: Quick Win Implementation ($1,000-3,000)

**What:** Implement one high-impact AI automation

**Examples:**
- Customer support chatbot (ChatGPT + knowledge base)
- Lead qualification agent (intake form → CRM)
- Document processing (invoices → accounting system)
- Email automation (smart replies, follow-ups)

**Deliverable:**
- Working prototype in 1-2 weeks
- Training documentation
- 30-day support

**Effort:** 10-20 hours

---

### Tier 3: Custom Agent Development ($3,000-10,000+)

**What:** Build bespoke AI agent for specific business need

**Examples:**
- Sales research agent (lead enrichment, competitive intel)
- Operations agent (scheduling, inventory, logistics)
- Compliance agent (document review, risk flagging)
- Content agent (research, drafting, optimization)

**Deliverable:**
- Custom agent with API integrations
- Full documentation
- 90-day support
- Training sessions

**Effort:** 40-100 hours
