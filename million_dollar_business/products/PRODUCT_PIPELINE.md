# 🏭 PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT PIPELINE

## Overview
Pipeline for creating, launching, and scaling digital products for our million-dollar business.

## Stages

### 1. IDEA GENERATION
**Goal:** Generate 50+ product ideas per week
**Methods:**
- Market research (web scraping, social media trends)
- Customer pain point analysis
- Competitor analysis
- AI brainstorming sessions

**Output:** Idea database with validation scores

### 2. VALIDATION
**Goal:** Test demand before building
**Methods:**
- Landing page tests
- Email list signups
- Social media polls
- Pre-order campaigns

**Metrics:**
- Conversion rate > 5%
- Email signups > 100
- Social engagement > 100 likes/shares

### 3. MVP DEVELOPMENT
**Goal:** Build minimum viable product
**Resources:**
- Product Development Agent (primary)
- Hoppscotch for API testing
- Free tools stack for infrastructure

**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks per MVP
**Budget:** $0 (using existing resources)

### 4. LAUNCH PREPARATION
**Goal:** Ready for customer acquisition
**Components:**
- Stripe payment setup
- Product delivery system
- Marketing materials
- Support documentation

### 5. LAUNCH & ITERATE
**Goal:** Launch and improve based on feedback
**Process:**
- Soft launch to 100 people
- Gather feedback
- Iterate quickly
- Scale marketing

## Development Standards

### Technical Standards:
- All products must work with Stripe
- Automated delivery on payment
- Mobile responsive
- SEO optimized

### Quality Standards:
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Comprehensive documentation
- Video tutorials
- Customer support ready

### Security Standards:
- No hardcoded API keys
- Environment variables only
- Regular security audits
- Data encryption

## Success Metrics

### Development Metrics:
- Time to MVP: < 2 weeks
- Bug rate: < 1 per 100 users
- Customer satisfaction: > 4.5/5

### Business Metrics:
- Customer acquisition cost: < 30% of price
- Conversion rate: > 3%
- Refund rate: < 5%
