# Deal Origination & Referral Fee System

AI-powered deal origination engine capturing economics at point of discovery.

## Dual-Sided Targeting

### Sellers (10-15/day)
- Off-market businesses only
- No business brokers
- $500K-$10M+ EBITDA
- Cash-flow blue-collar OR institutional platform targets

### Buyers (3-4/day)
- Private Equity firms
- Independent sponsors
- Family offices
- Search funds
- Must accept referral fee agreements

---

## Segment A: Cash-Flow Blue-Collar

**Target Profile:**
- $500K - $3M EBITDA
- Strong recurring cash flow
- Low customer concentration
- Owner-operated / nearing retirement

**Industries:**
- HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical
- Fire & Safety, Waste Management
- Commercial Cleaning, Roofing
- Niche Industrial Services

**Buyer Type:** Independent sponsors, search funds, family offices

---

## Segment B: Institutional Platform

**Target Profile:**
- $2M - $10M+ EBITDA
- Fragmented industry
- Platform-ready or tuck-in
- Scalable operational backbone

**Buyer Type:** PE firms, roll-up platforms, institutional investors

## Seller Sources (Off-Market)

1. **Google Maps** - Category clusters (HVAC, plumbing, etc.)
2. **Secretary of State** - Business filings, age >15 years
3. **SBA Loan Database** - Loan maturity signals
4. **Industry Directories** - Association memberships
5. **Review Platforms** - High reviews, low digital sophistication

**Signals:**
- Business age > 15 years
- Owner nearing retirement
- Outdated website/branding
- High review count, low marketing spend

---

## Buyer Sources

1. **PE Firm Databases** - PitchBook, Crunchbase
2. **Search Fund Networks** - Stanford, ETA communities
3. **Family Office Lists** - FOX, FINTRX
4. **LinkedIn** - Investment professionals by thesis
5. **Deal Platforms** - Axial, DealStream

**Qualification:**
- Active in target segments
- History of paying finder fees
- Responsive to off-market flow

---

## Referral Fee Structure

**Terms:**
- 5% up to $1M
- Sliding scale above $1M
- Minimum $50,000
- Paid upon closing only
- Includes deferred consideration
- Includes assumed debt
- 12-month tail

## Outreach Sequences

### Seller Outreach (Day 1)
**Subject:** Quick question about [Company Name]

Hi [Owner Name],

I noticed [Company Name] has been serving [area] for [X] years - impressive reputation.

I work with acquirers looking for well-run [industry] businesses like yours. No broker involved - just direct introductions to buyers who understand the value of what you've built.

If you've ever thought about what a transition might look like (not necessarily now, but someday), I'd be happy to share what similar businesses are fetching in today's market.

No pitch. Just market color.

[Signature]

---

### Buyer Outreach (Day 1)
**Subject:** Off-market [Industry] deal flow - finder fee arrangement?

Hi [Name],

I'm building a sourcing engine for off-market [segment] businesses:
- $500K-$10M EBITDA
- [Industries]
- Direct owner relationships, no brokers

I operate on a finder's fee basis (5% up to $1M, sliding scale, paid at close).

If you're open to receiving vetted deal flow with standard referral economics, I'd love to connect.

What types of opportunities are you actively chasing right now?

[Signature]

## Economic Model

- EBITDA: $1.5M
- Price: $5M
- Fee: ~$150K

**Institutional Deal:**
- Platform: $20M EV
- Fee: ~$300K-$500K

**Target:** 10 closings/year = $1.5M+ finder revenue
