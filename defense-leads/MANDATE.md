### Investment Criteria
The Group prioritizes:
- Strong technical and engineering capabilities.
- Complementary technologies enabling integration or co-development.
- Potential to unlock commercial and technological synergies.
- Access to new customers, markets, and defense agencies.

> **Note:** Revenue scale is not a primary determinant. Integration potential, IP ownership, and technical depth are central to evaluation.
